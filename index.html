Super Spiced Brothers!

Instructions

The goal of the game is to fight other players and avoid falling off the boundaries of the game screen.

The more you get hit, the lighter you become and easier it is to get knocked out of the screen!

If you "die", you lose one live. Each player starts with 8 lives.


*NOTE* The game supports up to 4 players at once.

Controls

Move with the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys.

Jump with the UP arrow key (press jump twice mid-air to do a double jump).

Punch and kick with A and D!
